About the App

PrivTrans is a powerful speech recognition app that supports real-time transcription in Chinese and English. All audio processing is done locally on your device, ensuring your privacy and security.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I get started?

A: After opening the app, select a recognition model and tap the record button to start speech recognition. You can also upload audio files for transcription.

Q: What languages are supported?

A: Currently supports Chinese and English speech recognition.

Q: Is audio data uploaded to servers?

A: No. All audio processing is done locally on your device. No data is uploaded to any server, ensuring complete privacy protection.

Q: Can I use it offline?

A: Yes, the app works completely offline and does not require an internet connection.

Q: How can I improve recognition accuracy?

A: We recommend using the app in a quiet environment, speaking clearly, and maintaining an appropriate distance from the microphone (about 15-30 cm).

Q: Can it transcribe long audio files?

A: Yes. You can process longer audio files using the file upload feature.
